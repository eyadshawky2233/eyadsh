About Me

A young mind
driven by curiosity

I'm Eyad Shawky, a young programmer and game developer from Egypt. My journey started with a simple fascination — how do the apps and games I love actually work? That question led me down a path of relentless learning and building.

From developing Minecraft mods and servers since 2021, to building AI-powered logistics engines and competing nationally in software innovation, I've always believed that the best way to learn is to create.

Today, I'm expanding into cybersecurity — learning to break things so I can build them better. I'm a graduate of Egypt's Digital Buds Initiative (DEBI), a bug bounty hunter, and someone who genuinely loves the craft of software.
